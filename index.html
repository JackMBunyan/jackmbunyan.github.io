About Me

Hi! I'm Jack, a third-year student at the University of Bristol where I am studying Mathematics and Computer Science. My interests are in backend development, financial mathematics and data science solutions, although I also have some experience as a full-stack developer. More recently, I have been interested in agent-based models to solve problems or to study their behaviour in relation to real world scenarios. Please use the above navigation bar to check out some of my projects or read my CV.

I am currently an active member of the Bristol Computer Science Society's committee as the Sports and Socials Officer. My role involves organising and running social and academic events for all students with an interest in Computer Science; planning and executing bar socials, quiz events and networking opportunities.
