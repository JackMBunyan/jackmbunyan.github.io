About Me

Hi! I'm Jack, a third-year student at the University of Bristol where I am studying Mathematics and Computer Science. My interests are in backend development and data science solutions, although I also have some experience as a full-stack developer. My other passions include coding events such as game jams and capture the flag challenges.

I am currently an active member of the Bristol Computer Science Society's committee as the Sports and Socials Officer. My role involves organising and running social and academic events for all students with an interest in Computer Science; in particular, I have hosted multiple quiz nights and planned pub crawls.
